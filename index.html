WELCOME TO HIT THE BAT GAME PAGE!!!!

HEY GAMERS! Your character in this game is as a famous Baseball player. A cauldron of bats has attacked you and now it is your only chance to "save everybody"!!

THE CONTROLS OF THE GAME:

To move up- UP ARROW
To move down- DOWN ARROW
To shoot baseball- SPACE

EACH BAT YOU HIT YOU WILL EARN 10 POINTS. If your character touches the bat then you lose the game.
